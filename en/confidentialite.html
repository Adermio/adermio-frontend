Data & AI

Privacy Policy

Updated for AI Improvement: January 2026

Trust is the foundation of our relationship. At Adermio, we use advanced artificial intelligence technologies to analyze your skin. This policy details how we handle your data to provide this service, and how we anonymize it to advance technological research, in strict compliance with the GDPR.

1. Data Collected

To provide our skin analysis service and improve our algorithms, we collect:

  • Face Photos: Source images required for AI analysis.
  • Skin Data: Questionnaire responses (age, skin type, current routine).
  • Technical Data: Error logs and analysis results to improve model accuracy.
  • Contact Details: Email for sending the PDF report.

2. Purpose of Processing

Your data is used for two distinct objectives:

A. Service Delivery

Analyzing your skin in real-time, generating your personalized report, and providing customer support.

B. AI Improvement (R&D)

Training, testing, and refining Adermio's machine learning models using de-identified and aggregated data.

3. Retention & Archiving

We apply a strict differentiated retention policy:

  • Identifiable Data (User Account):
    Raw photos linked to your email are kept for 30 days (to allow for support or report regeneration), after which they are either deleted or anonymized.
  • Training Data (Anonymized):
    Once the link to your identity is broken (anonymization), images and skin data are integrated into our research database ("Dataset"). This anonymous data is kept indefinitely to allow our algorithms to evolve over time.

4. Anonymization Process

Before any use for AI training, your data undergoes irreversible processing:

  • Removal of all metadata (IP, Date, GPS location, Device ID).
  • Total dissociation from your email address and name.
  • Assignment of a unique random identifier for statistical study.

5. Data Sharing

Adermio never sells your personal data to third parties for advertising purposes.

Anonymized data may be processed on secure servers of our technology partners specializing in AI (cloud computing), under strict confidentiality agreements.

6. Your Rights and Consent

In accordance with GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, and erase your personal data.

Important: You can request the deletion of your identifiable photos at any time. However, once your data has been anonymized and integrated into our AI models (Dataset), it becomes technically impossible to identify, isolate, or delete it, as it is no longer linked to your person.
